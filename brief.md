About Me

Hello, I’m Purdi.

I am a registered nurse with over 15 years of NHS and private healthcare experience and the founder of Cottage Aesthetics, which has proudly welcomed clients for the past 18 months.

Throughout my nursing career I have worked within Plastic Surgery, Dermatology and specialist reconstructive services, caring for patients undergoing life-changing procedures following cancer, trauma and congenital conditions. Working alongside highly skilled plastic surgeons and multidisciplinary teams gave me an exceptional understanding of skin health, wound healing, facial anatomy and the importance of precision, symmetry and attention to detail.

These specialist environments taught me that every patient is unique. Whether reconstructing following illness or injury, or enhancing natural features through aesthetic medicine, there is no one-size-fits-all approach. The very best outcomes come from understanding the individual in front of you, respecting their natural features and creating results that look balanced, refreshed and authentically them.

My nursing career has also shown me that great care extends far beyond the treatment itself. Trust, honesty, safety and communication are at the heart of every positive patient journey. I believe clients should fully understand their treatment options, feel confident in their practitioner and know they are receiving care based on knowledge, experience and evidence.

These values inspired me to create Cottage Aesthetics—a peaceful countryside clinic in Hartlebury where clients can enjoy a calm, private experience away from the busy high street. Here, appointments are never rushed, treatments are tailored to the individual and natural-looking results are always the priority.

As a registered nurse, I continue to maintain my registration with the Nursing and Midwifery Council (NMC), hold comprehensive medical indemnity insurance and regularly undertake continuing professional development to ensure my knowledge remains current and evidence based.

I have completed advanced training in all of the treatments I offer and am currently undertaking a Level 7 qualification in Aesthetic Medicine. Although not currently a legal requirement, I believe investing in the highest level of education reflects my commitment to clinical excellence, patient safety and providing the very best care for every client.

My goal has always been simple: to help you look and feel like the best version of yourself—never changing who you are, simply enhancing the features that make you uniquely you.
