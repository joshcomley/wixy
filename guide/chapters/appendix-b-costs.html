What a typical month looks like

Expect somewhere around £10–15 all in, most months — the droplet plus modest AI usage. Ordinary use of the AI assistant (a handful of content changes a month) tends to cost a small fraction of the $40 cap; that cap is a ceiling for an unusually heavy month, not a bill you should expect to see.

What goes away

Once you're confident — after chapter 8, and however long afterwards feels right — you can cancel your existing Wix subscription. Most Wix Business or Ecommerce plans cost more per month than the £9.50 droplet above, so for many site owners this change is a small overall saving, not an added cost, once that old subscription is gone.
